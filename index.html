About The Company

ReformedTech is an emerging software startup with a global clientele. We specialize in strategic consulting, web and app development, and technical solutions. Our expertise lies in crafting cutting-edge, tailored solutions for clients, backed by a proven track record of successful project deliveries. Our commitment to exemplary customer service and staying at the forefront of technology sets us apart in the competitive industry.

Job Context:

We are looking for highly motivated engineers who want to solve challenging technical challenges and learn and incorporate new technologies into their skillset. You would use various tech stacks in this role, including PHP, Laravel & VueJS/ReactJS. You will be engaged across the software development life cycle to create and modify platforms and capabilities in a collaborative and Agile environment. You will work in development teams` efforts to assess high-level design issues relating to platform, enterprise software, and interactions with other systems and participate in architecture and design sessions.

Good command of the English language is a must.

Job Responsibilities

  • Lead a team of developers in the design, development, and implementation of web applications and software solutions.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, define project scope, and establish timelines.
  • Architect scalable and maintainable codebases that adhere to best practices and coding standards.
  • Mentor and coach team members, providing guidance and support to foster professional growth and development.
  • Conduct code reviews and ensure quality assurance processes are followed to deliver high-quality products.
  • Stay updated with the latest technologies and trends in web development, and drive innovation within the team.

Requirements

  • Proven experience (5+ years) as a Full Stack Developer, with expertise in both frontend and backend development.
  • Strong proficiency in frontend technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js).
  • Extensive experience with server-side languages and frameworks, such as PHP - Laravel, Node.js or similar.
  • Solid understanding of relational and non-relational databases (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL).
  • Experience in working freelance marketplace will be a plus.
  • Experience in WordPress and Shopify will get high priority.
  • Experience working with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform.
  • Prior experience in a leadership or managerial role, with the ability to lead and inspire a team.
  • Excellent communication skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities effectively.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with microservices architecture and RESTful API design principles.
  • Contributions to open-source projects or participation in developer communities.

Educational Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree from any reputed local or foreign university.

Salary

  • Salary: 60k-70k Tk. Monthly
